Let $u$ and $v$ be two derivable functions:
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
 & \begin{split}
        \int_{x_0}^{x_1}\left(u v\right)'\dd x & = \int_{x_0}^{x_1}\left(u' v + u v' \right)\dd x \\
                                               & = u v|_{x_1} - u v|_{x_0} \\
   \end{split}\\
\Rightarrow &
 \int_{x_0}^{x_1}\left(u' v\right) \dd x = u v|_{x_1} - u v|_{x_0} - \int_{x_0}^{x_1}\left( u v'\right) \dd x
\end{split}
\label{sec:math:ipp}
\end{equation}
