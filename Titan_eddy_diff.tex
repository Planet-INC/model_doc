Eddy diffusion 
\begin{equation}
\eddy = \eddy_0  \sqrt{\frac{\concAtm(\zmin)}{\concAtm(z)}}
\label{Titan:eddy_diff}
\end{equation}
Thus
\begin{equation}
\doverdz{\eddy} = -\frac{1}{2} \eddy_0  \frac{\sqrt{\concAtm(\zmin)}}{\concAtm\sqrt{\concAtm}}\diverge{\conc}
\label{Titan:eddy_diff_deriv}
\end{equation}
